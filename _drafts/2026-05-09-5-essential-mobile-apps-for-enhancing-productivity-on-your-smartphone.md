## What’s the Difference Between Evernote and Notion?

When comparing Evernote and Notion, the primary difference lies in their structure. Evernote is primarily a note-taking application, while Notion is an all-in-one workspace that combines notes with task management and project planning.

**Evernote** 
- **Functionality**: Strong note-taking capabilities.
- **Extras**: Searchable text, web clipping, and integration with other apps.
- **Pricing**: Free version available; premium at $7.99/month.

**Notion** 
- **Functionality**: A platform for notes, projects, and databases.
- **Extras**: Customizable templates and ability to share with teams.
- **Pricing**: Free for individual use; $5/month for teams.

Notably, I found Notion’s versatility to be a game changer for my daily tasks—combining my notes with project boards streamlined my workflow.
