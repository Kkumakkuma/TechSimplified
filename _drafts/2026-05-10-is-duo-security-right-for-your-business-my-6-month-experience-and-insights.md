## How Does Duo Security Work?
Duo Security employs a multi-factor authentication (MFA) approach, enabling organizations to add an additional layer of security beyond just usernames and passwords. Here’s how it operates:

1. **User Authentication:** When a user logs in, they will enter their credentials as usual. Duo then triggers an additional verification method.
   
2. **Verification Methods:** Users can choose various authentication methods such as push notifications to a mobile device, text messages, or phone calls.
   
3. **Security Policies:** Organizations can customize authentication policies based on user roles and access needs, ensuring that sensitive areas require stricter verification.

These multi-layered protections not only fortify user accounts but also instill a deeper level of security throughout the organization. According to a report by the Cybersecurity & Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA) from late 2022, MFA can prevent up to 99.9% of account compromise attacks.
