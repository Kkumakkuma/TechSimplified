Over the past year, I’ve sent and received thousands of emails via ProtonMail, with the end-to-end encryption giving me substantial peace of mind. My fears about phishing attacks dwindled as I felt far less vulnerable than when using generic email services. At just $1.33 a month for their Premium plan, Tutanota is the budget-friendly alternative that caught my interest in mid-2026.
