AI in content marketing uses machine learning algorithms to analyze data, generate content ideas, and optimize distribution channels. What does this look like in practice? I tested a content creation tool that generates blog outlines based on trending topics. Within a week, I noticed my writing process sped up significantly, cutting down my time from hours to just 30 minutes for initial drafts.
