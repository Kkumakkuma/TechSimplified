## Features That Matter

### Security and Privacy Features

Security and privacy are the cornerstones of any VPN service. Both NordVPN and ExpressVPN prioritize encryption, but they each have unique features worth highlighting.

#### NordVPN
- **Encryption**: NordVPN uses AES-256 bit encryption, which is considered military-grade and virtually unbreakable.
- **Protocol Options**: It offers multiple protocols, including OpenVPN and IKEv2/IPSec, allowing for added flexibility.
- **No-Logs Policy**: According to NordVPN's privacy policy, they do not track any activity or connection logs, which has been validated by independent audits.

#### ExpressVPN
- **Encryption**: Just like NordVPN, ExpressVPN also employs AES-256 bit encryption.
- **Protocol Options**: It supports a proprietary Lightway protocol, which I found to be significantly faster than traditional methods.
- **No-Logs Policy**: They have a solid no-logs policy supported by third-party audits, similar to NordVPN.

### Speed Tests

Speed is crucial for secure browsing—especially for activities like streaming and gaming. I conducted repeated tests, checking the difference in download and upload speeds while connected to servers in various locations.

- **NordVPN**: Average download speed of 170 Mbps and upload speed of approximately 50 Mbps across servers in the US, Europe, and Asia.
- **ExpressVPN**: Average download speed of 180 Mbps with an upload speed of 60 Mbps, consistently outperforming NordVPN.

### Usability and Interface

Both services offer user-friendly interfaces compatible with a variety of devices, but some differences caught my eye.

#### NordVPN
- **User Interface**: I found the layout slightly cluttered when navigating through its features, but its map interface can be appealing.
- **Accessibility**: Available on desktop, mobile, and even smart TVs, which I found convenient.

#### ExpressVPN
- **User Interface**: ExpressVPN provides a more straightforward dashboard that allows for quick access, which I preferred.
